About Me

Hi! My name is Morgan, I am a recent graduate of Middlesex Community College where I studied Graphic Design. When I started school, I was unsure of the direction I wanted to take, so I went with what I knew best and enrolled in a drawing class.

Each time the class met, we would stand in a circle and critique the prior weeks assignment. During a routine critique, my Professor was talking about my piece when she walked over to me and asked, "Is this your field? Because I think this is your field." She was right, I knew I had to be an artist.

Not long afterwards, I switched my major to Graphic Design. With the help of my experienced professors and creative classmates, my design skills improved tremendously during my two years at Middlesex.
